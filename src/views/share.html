Sharing Data: How you share the user's data externally with other companies or entities

You share the user's identifiable data:

  • To provide the primary service of your product
  • To conduct scientific research
  • For company operations (e.g. quality control or fraud detection)
  • To develop and improve new and current products and services (e.g., analytics)
  • Other
  • You DO NOT share the user's identifiable data

You share the user's data AFTER removing identifiers (note that remaining data may still not be completely anonymous):

  • To provide the primary service of your product
  • To conduct scientific research
  • For company operations (e.g. quality control or fraud detection)
  • To develop and improve new and current products and services (e.g., analytics)
  • Other
  • You DO NOT share the user's data after removing identifiers
